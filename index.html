About

I am a Tech Lead with 15 years of work experience. I have been fortunate to have led or been deeply involved in multiple engineering projects over the past decade and a half, covering different layers of distributed backend architectures:

  • Infrastructure layer: Azure Pilotfish (formerly Bing Autopilot) infrastructure architecture.
  • Platform layer: Cluster resource management system based on Yarn and HDFS, as well as the Bing IndexServe service.
  • Application layer: Bing Entity Search and multiple end-to-end features for NewsBreak App

This profile page serves as a supplement to my LinkedIn page and resume. On the blog page, I will share stories about my development experiences, and I will also prepare some useful tips and information for those who need it.

Rather than the fleeting updates on large platforms like Facebook and LinkedIn, I prefer to use this space as a static index page, so that valuable knowledge and information won't disappear with the stream's flow.
